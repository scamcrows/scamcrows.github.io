New to Raids? Want to Learn?
Check out these two amazing communities below!!

EU


We are The Crossroads Inn [EU], a raid training community on discord. We run official raid trainings every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday at 18:00 GMT.
Trainings last at least 2 hours, but not more than 3!

What are the requirements to raid with us?
- At least one character geared with a meta build.
- We require ascended weapons & trinkets and exotic (or better) armor, as well as correct food and utility.
- You should have basic knowledge of your class and some understanding of its role in a raid.
- Be able to listen on discord. Talking is not required, but it is encouraged.
- You must be able to read and write in english, as well as understand spoken english.
- Be online and ready for squad invite at least 15 minutes before the start of raids.

NA


Guild Wars 2 Raid Academy [NA] is a Discord server that has been operating since June! We are now with over 2,700 active members. We provide a community setting that is built on the foundations of Growth, Respect, and Unity.

We want to help new players find a path to becoming legendary raiders, one step at a time. Additionally, we offer a space for seasoned veterans to find equally skilled players for weekly full clears while providing them the opportunity to share their experience as Trainers!

Our training methods are based upon on a Tier System. We categorize raid bosses into four distinct tiers based on the difficulty of learning and executing the encounter. We work with players to improve their knowledge of each individual boss encounter within a Tier, preparing them to eventually be able to Clear these bosses each week
